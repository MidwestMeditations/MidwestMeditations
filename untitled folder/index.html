About

Midwest Meditations is a creative studio rooted in personal loss, Midwest identity, and the evolution of my recovery through art and music. I’m the founder of Midwest Meditations, featured in The Wall Street Journal for insights on the nonalcoholic space—but that only scratched the surface.

Through digital storytelling, visual design, and meaningful community building, I explore what it means to rebuild with purpose, privacy, and presence. This project is both personal and communal—an open space for reflection, connection, and creative recovery.This is a personal, visual, and reflective brand built from grief, Midwest grit, and the spirit of creative resilience. I blend my cybersecurity experience with a sharp eye for visual storytelling and privacy-conscious digital strategy.

Midwest Meditations is more than content—it’s recovery, nature, and community, all grounded in place and purpose.
